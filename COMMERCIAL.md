## Licensing Options

### 1. One-Time Commercial License
Pay once, use forever for specified use case.

**Pricing based on:**
- Company size
- Expected user base
- Deployment scope
- Support requirements

### 2. Revenue-Sharing Agreement
Partner with the project, share in the success.

**Structure:**
- X% of gross revenue from products using TCOD
- Quarterly reporting and payments
- Long-term partnership model
- Mutual growth incentive

### 3. Equity/Partnership
For startups and strategic ventures.

**Includes:**
- Equity stake in your company, OR
- Co-ownership of joint venture
- Active technical involvement
- Shared vision and roadmap

### 4. White-Label Licensing
Rebrand and integrate into your product.

**Features:**
- Remove TCOD branding
- Custom UI/UX
- Priority feature development
- Dedicated support

### 5. Enterprise License
For large organizations.

**Includes:**
- Unlimited internal use
- On-premise deployment rights
- Custom SLA and support
- Training and onboarding

## Philosophy

I believe in **fair compensation for value created**. If you're making money using my work, I should benefit too. But I'm flexible and creative about deal structures.

**I prefer:**
- Long-term partnerships over one-off deals
- Revenue sharing over large upfront payments (shows confidence)
- Collaboration over arm's-length licensing
- Win-win scenarios over zero-sum negotiations
